Future Project Ideas

Here are a couple of ideas for new apps. Your feedback is welcome!

Cognitive Bias Explorer

An interactive educational app to help users learn about and identify common cognitive biases. The goal is to improve critical thinking and decision-making.

Key Features:

  • Explore a library of common cognitive biases.
  • Read clear definitions and real-world examples.
  • Test your understanding with interactive quizzes.
  • Learn tips to recognize and mitigate biases in daily life.

Simple Pomodoro Timer

A minimalist timer to help you practice the Pomodoro Technique for improved focus and productivity. Work in focused 25-minute intervals with short breaks.

Key Features:

  • Clean, distraction-free timer display.
  • Automatic switching between work and break intervals.
  • Sound notifications to signal session ends.
  • Track your completed Pomodoro sessions.

Daily Stoic

A simple app that displays a new Stoic quote each day, with a short reflection prompt to encourage mindfulness.

Key Features:

  • A new Stoic quote every day.
  • A short daily reflection or journal prompt.
  • A clean, minimalist interface.

Mental Model Primer

An educational app that introduces users to various mental models to improve their thinking.

Key Features:

  • Learn about mental models like First-Principles Thinking, Occam's Razor, and Inversion.
  • Interactive exercises for each model.
  • Simple explanations and examples.

History of Philosophy Timeline

An interactive timeline of the history of philosophy, from the pre-Socratics to the modern day.

Key Features:

  • Explore key philosophers and their ideas.
  • Filter by philosophical period.
  • A clean and simple interface for learning.

Habit Tracker

A utility app to help you build and track daily habits.

Key Features:

  • Create a list of daily habits.
  • Check off habits each day.
  • Track your progress with simple visualizations.

Book Notes

A simple tool for taking and organizing notes from books.

Key Features:

  • Create entries for each book.
  • Add notes and tag them with keywords.
  • A clean and simple interface for organizing your thoughts.

Workout Generator

A simple app that generates random workouts based on your preferences.

Key Features:

  • Choose your workout type (bodyweight, dumbbells, cardio).
  • Get a new workout every time.
  • Simple and easy-to-follow exercises.

Mindfulness Bell

A simple app that plays a calming bell sound at regular intervals to help you practice mindfulness.

Key Features:

  • Set the interval for the bell.
  • Choose from a selection of calming sounds.
  • A minimalist interface to avoid distractions.

Decision Journal

A tool for making better decisions by recording your reasoning and reviewing the outcomes.

Key Features:

  • Record the context and reasoning behind important decisions.
  • Set a future date to review the outcome.
  • Learn from your past decisions to make better ones in the future.

Vocabulary Builder

A simple app for learning new words.

Key Features:

  • A new word each day with its definition and synonyms.
  • Example sentences to see the word in context.
  • A simple and engaging way to expand your vocabulary.

Evolutionary Concepts Explorer

An interactive app to learn about core evolutionary concepts like natural selection, genetic drift, and speciation with simulations.

Key Features:

  • Interactive simulations of evolutionary processes.
  • Clear explanations of key concepts.
  • Quizzes to test your understanding.

Probability Simulator

A tool to visualize and understand probability distributions (e.g., binomial, normal) by running simulations.

Key Features:

  • Visualize common probability distributions.
  • Run simulations to see probability in action.
  • Adjust parameters and see the effects.

Statistical Fallacy Spotter

An educational app that teaches users to identify common statistical fallacies in news and media.

Key Features:

  • Library of common statistical fallacies.
  • Real-world examples from news and media.
  • Interactive quizzes to test your skills.

Evolutionary Psychology Primer

An app that explains key concepts from evolutionary psychology, like parental investment theory and reciprocal altruism.

Key Features:

  • Clear explanations of key theories.
  • Case studies and examples from human and animal behavior.
  • Links to further reading and resources.

Bayesian Thinking Calculator

A tool to help users apply Bayes' theorem to everyday problems and update their beliefs based on new evidence.

Key Features:

  • A simple calculator for Bayes' theorem.
  • Guided examples for common scenarios.
  • Track how your beliefs change with new evidence.

Genetic Algorithm Playground

A sandbox to create and experiment with simple genetic algorithms to solve optimization problems.

Key Features:

  • Visualize genetic algorithms in action.
  • Tweak parameters like mutation rate and population size.
  • Solve classic problems like the traveling salesman.

Mating Strategies Explorer

An interactive guide to different mating strategies in the animal kingdom, explained through the lens of evolutionary biology.

Key Features:

  • Explore monogamy, polygamy, and other strategies.
  • Learn about sexual selection and its consequences.
  • Rich visuals and examples from the animal kingdom.

Cognitive Bias & Evolutionary Origins

An extension of the cognitive bias app that explains the potential evolutionary origins of each bias.

Key Features:

  • Connect cognitive biases to their evolutionary roots.
  • Understand the "why" behind our irrational thinking.
  • Side-by-side comparison of bias and its origin.

Statistical Significance Simulator

An app that demonstrates the concept of p-values and statistical significance through interactive examples.

Key Features:

  • Visualize p-values and significance levels.
  • Run simulated experiments to see how sample size affects significance.
  • Learn about common misinterpretations of p-values.

Human Evolution Timeline

An interactive timeline of human evolution, from early hominins to modern Homo sapiens.

Key Features:

  • Explore key milestones in human evolution.
  • View 3D models of hominin skulls.
  • Learn about the major hominin species.

Altruism Simulator

A simulation tool to explore the evolution of altruism using models like the Prisoner's Dilemma.

Key Features:

  • Play the Prisoner's Dilemma against different strategies.
  • Simulate the evolution of cooperation in a population.
  • Learn about tit-for-tat and other successful strategies.

Correlation vs. Causation Game

A game that challenges players to distinguish between correlation and causation in various scenarios.

Key Features:

  • Real and funny examples of spurious correlations.
  • Learn the criteria for establishing causation.
  • A fun way to improve critical thinking.

Life History Theory Explorer

An app explaining life history theory and how organisms allocate resources to growth, maintenance, and reproduction.

Key Features:

  • Learn about r/K selection theory.
  • Explore trade-offs in life history strategies.
  • Compare the life histories of different species.

Monte Carlo Simulation Tool

A simple tool for running Monte Carlo simulations for basic risk analysis and prediction.

Key Features:

  • Define variables and their probability distributions.

  • Visualize the distribution of possible outcomes.

Evolution of Cooperation Visualizer

An interactive visualization of how cooperation can evolve and be maintained in a population.

Key Features:

  • Watch cooperation evolve in a simulated population.
  • Experiment with different game theory models.
  • Understand the conditions that favor cooperation.

Key Features:
